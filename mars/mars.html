Mars

Mars, also known as the Red Planet, is the fourth planet from the Sun. It has the tallest volcano and the deepest canyon in the Solar System.

Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mostly of carbon dioxide. It is home to the largest volcano, Olympus Mons, and the deepest canyon, Valles Marineris.

  • 🔴 Mars has a reddish color due to iron oxide (rust) on its surface.
  • 🛰️ NASA's Perseverance Rover is currently exploring Mars.
  • ❄️ Mars has polar ice caps made of water and carbon dioxide.
  • 🌋 Olympus Mons is nearly three times taller than Mount Everest.
  • 👽 Scientists are searching for signs of ancient life on Mars.
