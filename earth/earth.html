Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body that supports life. It has a rich atmosphere, vast oceans, and diverse ecosystems.

Earth is unique because it has liquid water, oxygen-rich air, and the perfect conditions for life. It has one natural satellite, the Moon, which influences its tides.

  • 🌊 Over 70% of Earth's surface is covered by water.
  • 🌍 Earth is approximately 4.5 billion years old.
  • 🌪️ It has an atmosphere composed mainly of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%).
  • 🌕 Earth’s Moon is about 1/6th the size of Earth.
  • 🌿 It is home to millions of species, making it the most biodiverse planet known.
