☀️ The Sun - The Heart of Our Solar System

A massive glowing ball of hot plasma that powers life on Earth.

The Sun is a gigantic, nuclear-fusion-powered star that provides light, heat, and energy to our entire Solar System. It is made mostly of hydrogen and helium.

  • ☀️ Diameter: 1,391,000 km
  • 🔥 Surface Temperature: 5,500°C
  • 🌟 Core Temperature: 15 million°C
  • 🌍 Provides 99.8% of the Solar System's mass
